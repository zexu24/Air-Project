Frequently Asked Questions

How can we personally bring
change to Air Pollution?

That's a great question. Being proactive and mindful of our energy and water consumption is a step in the right direction. We recommend utilizing public transporation to limit carbon emissions.

Anything else that we should know?

The general idea is to switch to renewable energy; using solar panels and wind turbines to generate energy are a few ways we can reduce air pollution. Participating in your local advocacy groups are also a great way to spread awareness and spark change in policies.
